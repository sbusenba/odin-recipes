Joe's Special

Description

A recipe inspired by San Francisco's Joe's restraunt

Ingredients

  • Spinach (2 bags)
  • Ground Beef (2 lbs)
  • Eggs (2)
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Mushrooms
  • Parmasean Cheese (1 cup)
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef.
  2. Add onions
  3. Add Spinach,Mushrooms, Parmesean, and 2 eggs
  4. stir and heat
