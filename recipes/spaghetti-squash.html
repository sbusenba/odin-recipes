Spaghetti Squash

Description

A delicious alternative to pasta

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Squash
  • Ground Beef (1 lb)
  • Spaghetti Sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400
  2. Brown Ground Beef and drain.
  3. Add Spaghetti Sauce
  4. Slice Spaghetti Squash in half lengthwise
  5. Place Spaghetti Squash face down in pan with one inch of water
  6. Cook in oven for 25 minutes.
  7. Shred spaghetti Squash with fork. Top with Sauce.
