Cauliflower Pizza

Description

This is a delicious pizza with few carbs due to it's cauliflower crust

Ingredients

  • Cauliflower (1 head)
  • Pizza Sauce (1/4th cup)
  • Eggs (2)
  • Pepperoni
  • Mozzarella (2 cups)
  • Parmasean Cheese (1 cup)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees
  2. Grate Cauliflower, and steam
  3. Add 1/2 cup Mozzarella, and 2 egg whites
  4. Flatten into pizza pan, bake 10 minutes on each side
  5. add 1/4 cup pizza sauce, then pepperoni, and the remainder of the Cheese.
